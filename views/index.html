Bombay Stock Exchange

The Bombay Stock Exchange(BSE) became the first stock exchange to be recognized by the Indian Government under the Securities Contracts Regulation Act. The BSE is the world's 11th largest stock exchange with an overall market capitalization of $1.43 Trillion as of March, 2016. More than 5500 companies are publicly listed on the BSE. New York Stock Exchange

The New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) is by far the world's largest stock exchange by market capitalization of its listed companies at US$19.3 trillion as of June 2016. The NYSE is owned by Intercontinental Exchange, an American holding company that it also lists. The New York Stock Exchange provides a means for buyers and sellers to trade shares of stock in companies registered for public trading.
